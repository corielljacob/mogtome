# Privacy Policy
_Last updated: 01/16/2025_

## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how **MogTome** (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

By using the application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Discord User Data
When you login to MogTome with discord, we will receive the following information from Discord:

- User ID
- Username and discriminator
- Avatar
- FC rank and other FC-specific data about your character

We will not obtain any other information from discord than this.

## How We Use Your Information
We will **only** use your information to validate you as an FC member and show you information only visibile to FC members.
We do **not** and will **never** sell, distribute, or manipulate your data in any form, to any other parties.

## Data Storage and Security
Data is stored in a secure database in which only authorized systems/services can access stored data.

## Removing Your Data
Users may request deletion of their data by:

- Contacting any Moogle Knight in the Kupo Life! Free Company
- Revoking the application from Discord’s “Authorized Apps” page

Once revoked, all associated data will be deleted within **3 days**.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.  
Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
